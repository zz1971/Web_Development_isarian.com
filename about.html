Armin Isarian (born April 14, 1970)is an Iranian director, producer, and screenwriter. He graduated from Alborz College. He studied filmmaking at the University of Science and Culture and then spent four years studying Western Philosophy at Islamic Azad University. During the time he has been making films, he has also taught filmmaking – screenwriting in different universities and institutes such as Iranian Youth Cinema Society (2005–present), University of Applied Science and Technology(2012-2013) ,and Maktab Tehran School of Art (2013-2015) .
